On The Mat

For many people, Yoga can be fun as a hobby ie. great to maintain a healthy physic,
and good posture. However others practice yoga as a meditating exercise, that allows one
to calm the senses and work towards a healthy mind.

The yoga community can act as a place to empower those who choose to be a part of it and embrace it. Just like any other sport, practicing yoga can act as a great method to release endorphins naturally. Endorphin is natural pain reliever, as well as a booster for happiness and pleasure.
