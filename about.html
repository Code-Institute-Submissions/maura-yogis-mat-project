About Us

The Yoga Mat, is a local and independent organisation, created to introduce Yoga to new people, and expand the yoga community in the city of Manchester, England.
The website is run by a group of friendly Yoga teachers and enthusiasts based in Manchester. We encourage everyone to keep a healthy and active lifestyle; therefore participate in activities that work toward maintaining the body strong and agile
